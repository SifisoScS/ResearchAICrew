Research Report: Machine Learning in Healthcare
Hypothesis: Can Machine Learning in Healthcare consistently achieve 20.0%+ efficiency with ML?
Findings: Analysis of 5 records shows average 20.0% efficiency gain. Linear model (R²=0.97) predicts 21.5% gain for 14 hours. Recommendation: Increase training hours to 14+ for optimal efficiency.
Conclusion: Promising results warrant further study.
